pHILOSOPHY

The human form is a paradox. We can survive the coldest winters and hottest summers, and yet, we cannot survive long without our life- blood: potato chips. When Professor Protzel and his team of psycho-filmologists approached us to create a short film that encapsulated the human condition, it seemed natural therefore to explore the emotions surrounding the potato chip.
